Inline Editing

The first open source publishing platform to feature inline editing. No more forms or rich-text editors. It’s the most immersive editing experience you’ll ever have!

Easy to Install

Upload it and open your browser. The installer will take care of the rest. Built on technologies you know and love. Got PHP/MySQL? You can run Leafpub.

Fully Responsive

Leafpub works on your desktop, laptop, tablet, and phone. There’s no app to download. Just a revolutionary, responsive admin panel with a beautiful interface.
